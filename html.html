Looks like it’s about time to renew your library card! 

For Town of Chapel Hill and Orange County residents, we renew cards every three years to ensure we have updated contact information for our patrons.  For our Out-of-County users, Chapel Hill-Carborro City School teachers, and UNC students, we renew annually. 

To renew your card, please see a staff member next time you’re in the Library with some ID showing your name and current address. That’s it! Town of Chapel Hill and Orange County residents will be good to go for another three years; another year for all our other patrons. Please note, Out-of-County renewals are subject to the $65 annual fee. Should you need immediate assistance, you can give us a call at 919-968-2777. Please do NOT reply to this email.

Thank you for helping us keep our records current and we look forward to seeing you soon!
